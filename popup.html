Client Request:

ONE, a global container shipping
company headquartered in Singapore, needed
a practical outerwear solution for
their employees that could be
utilized in both indoor and outdoor settings.

ShopsGifts Solution:

In response, we designed an exclusive
Reversible Parka/Hoodie.
Here’s how it meets their needs:

Dual Functionality:

The hoodie is fully reversible, featuring one side
made of soft cotton for indoor use,
perfect for air-conditioned environments.
The other side is crafted from a
water-resistant fabric, making it ideal
for outdoor wear in rainy conditions.

Versatile Wear:

Employees can easily switch between styles,
ensuring comfort whether they’re indoors or outside.

Global Fulfillment:

Recognizing ONE’s international presence,
we also provided storage and fulfillment services for their
overseas counterparts, ensuring seamless distribution of the outerwear.
This solution not only addresses their
practical needs but also enhances employee
comfort and brand unity across different regions.
